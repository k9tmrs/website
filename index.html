Innovative timing for Hoopers & dog sports

Welcome to K9 TMRS – The Affordable Timing Solution for Dog Sports

K9 TMRS (K9 Time Measuring & Recording System) is an innovative timing system designed for competitive dog sports, with a special focus on Hoopers. Our system leverages your smartphone to deliver accurate, real-time timing at a fraction of the cost of traditional systems.

How It Works

Simple, Smartphone-Powered Timing

K9 TMRS makes timing dog sports events simple and accessible. Using your own Android or iOS smartphone, you can display timing information directly on your device. Our intuitive setup process ensures you’re ready to go in minutes.

  1. 1

    Download the K9 TMRS app (coming soon)

    Install the upcoming K9 TMRS app on your Android or iOS smartphone to unlock powerful, portable timing capabilities.

  2. 2

    Connect your device to the timing system

    Pair your smartphone with the K9 TMRS timing hardware so you can control runs and monitor results in real time.

  3. 3

    Start timing your event and view results in real time

    Run your classes, capture accurate times, and see results instantly on your device and for your show processor.
